# Group Name: Libertarian
# Score Pattern: 5,1,5,4,2,2,2,1,4,1
# Description:
You believe strongly in personal agency and that humans are ultimately free.
Your worldview suggests that no external force fully determines who we are.
You likely believe that people are morally responsible for all their actions.

# Group Name: Hard Determinist
# Score Pattern: 1,5,2,2,5,2,5,5,3,5
# Description:
You view life as shaped by biology, environment, and history.
You believe that free will is mostly — or entirely — an illusion.
You may be skeptical of moral blame and see most behaviour as conditioned.

# Group Name: Compatibilist
# Score Pattern: 3,4,4,5,4,5,3,4,4,2
# Description:
You believe that free will and determinism can co-exist.
People can be influenced by causes but still held accountable.
You likely see life as a mix of personal agency and deep forces.

# Group Name: Moral Rationalist
# Score Pattern: 3,2,3,5,3,2,3,3,5,3
# Description:
You focus on moral responsibility, regardless of cause.
You believe people should be accountable for their actions — always.
Whether free or not, society must judge right and wrong.

# Group Name: Agnostic Explorer
# Score Pattern: 3,3,3,3,3,3,3,3,3,3
# Description:
You see the debate as complex and context-dependent.
You lean toward balance or uncertainty in your outlook.
You’re open to multiple interpretations — perhaps even paradoxes.
